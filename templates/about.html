The Irony Detector


This system provides information about whether the irony that we use verbally at every moment of our life is used in written text or how much irony it contains. The irony is above all a symbolic language. The words in the produced language undergo a semantic change. The fact that the word is not used in real terms takes irony into the use of a symbolic language and puts it into a figurative saying.
